Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2010, Telami Teachers and Business Training Centre has grown from a humble training center to a premier college in Nairobi.

Our commitment is to excellence in education and character building. We believe in empowering our students not just with academic knowledge, but with the moral compass and practical skills needed to thrive in today's dynamic world.

We are fully accredited by TVETA and KNEC, ensuring that your qualifications are recognized nationally and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and market-driven teacher training and business education that equips students with practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and ethical values for professional success.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in teacher training and business education in Kenya, recognized for producing competent, innovative, and ethical professionals who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in teaching
  • Integrity & Ethics
  • Innovation
  • Student-centered
  • Community engagement
