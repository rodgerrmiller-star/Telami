About Telami Teachers and Business Training Centre

A legacy of excellence in education since 2010.

Telami Teachers and Business Training Centre has grown from a small training center to a premier college in Nairobi, Kenya. Our commitment is to holistic education that builds character and competence.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and market-driven training that equips students with practical skills and ethical values.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for producing competent, innovative, and ethical professionals.
